Blacken cajun chicken

Description

Cajun Blackened Chicken is a delicious, spicy, quick and easy chicken dish that is a perfect recipe for Mardi Gras or anytime you want a quick, easy, and delicious dinner.

Ingredients

  • 5 Tbsp vegetable or canola oil
  • 4 Chicken breasts
  • 3-4 Tbsp ground Cajun seasoning
  • 1/2 small white onion finely diced
  • 1 rib celery diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 1/2 cups brown rice
  • 1/2 Tbsp gumbo
  • 1/2 Tbsp ground Cajun seasoning
  • 3-4 cups chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1 cup okra
  • 6 oz smoked sausage

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, stir together the first 9 ingredients.
  2. Spray both sides of the chicken breasts with cooking spray.
  3. Then sprinkle the seasoning over both sides of each chicken breast.
  4. In a large skillet over medium heat, add a tablespoon of olive oil. When the oil is hot, add the chicken breasts.
  5. Cover and cook the chicken breasts for approximately seven to eight minutes on each side.
  6. After the chicken has finished cooking, remove it from the heat and let it rest for about 5 to 10 minutes.
